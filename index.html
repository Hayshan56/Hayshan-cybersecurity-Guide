What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats.

With increasing cybercrime, the need for professionals in this field is at an all-time high.

Networking Basics for Cybersecurity

Understanding networking is crucial for cybersecurity professionals. Here are the key topics to learn:

  • OSI Model and TCP/IP
  • Subnetting and IP Addressing
  • DNS, DHCP, and NAT
  • Firewalls, VPNs, and Proxies
  • Network Protocols (HTTP, HTTPS, FTP, SSH, etc.)

Why Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is one of the most crucial fields today, protecting businesses, governments, and individuals from cyber threats.

  • High Demand: Companies worldwide are facing a shortage of cybersecurity professionals.
  • Career Growth: Cybersecurity offers multiple career paths, including ethical hacking, SOC analysis, and forensics.
  • Impactful Work: Protect organizations from data breaches, cyberattacks, and financial losses.
  • High Salaries: Due to the increasing need, cybersecurity roles offer competitive salaries.
  • Always Evolving: The field is dynamic, providing constant learning opportunities.
